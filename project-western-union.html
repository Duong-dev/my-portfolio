Fintech Remittance Mobile App

Western Union Remittance

MoMo 2022

Problem Statement

MoMo users needed a seamless, secure, and fast way to receive international remittances via Western Union directly through the app without visiting physical counters or dealing with complex paperwork.

Solution Approach

Led comprehensive business analysis for Western Union remittance integration, delivering stakeholder coordination, API specifications, and flow diagrams to establish a smooth, digital-first receiving experience.

Mobile Interface

Key Features

  • Remittance Code Input: Simple interface for entering Western Union transfer codes.
  • 3-Step Process: Streamlined flow - share MoMo info, sender uses service, receive notification and money.
  • Real-time Updates: Instant notifications when transfers are ready for pickup.
  • Integrated Experience: Seamlessly built into MoMo's main app interface.

User Experience

  • Vietnamese language support with clear, simple instructions.
  • Visual step-by-step guide for first-time users.
  • 24/7 availability with comprehensive support.

My Contributions

Stakeholder Collaboration

  • Worked with the Product Owner and Frontend team to design an optimized UI/UX tailored for money transfer flows.
  • Coordinated with Western Union to understand remittance business rules and their technical and UX requirements for partner integration.

Requirement Documentation

  • Authored the SRS (Software Requirements Specification) to define behaviors, edge cases, and functional logic.
  • Created internal API specifications to guide Frontend and Backend development.
  • Developed Sequence Diagrams to visualize system-level flows and Activity Diagrams to capture user-facing remittance processes.

Project Coordination

  • Defined User Stories in Jira and assigned tasks to FE, BE, and QA teams.
  • Collaborated with engineers to estimate and manage the project timeline based on resource capacity.

Product Validation

  • Led user acceptance testing and validated all app flows aligned with business and partner requirements.

Outcome

Successfully launched the Western Union remittance feature on the MoMo app.

Enabled users to receive money transfers from abroad in a fast, secure, and fully digital experience.

Delivered a product compliant with partner standards while maintaining MoMo's high usability benchmark.
