Travel Care Insurance

MoMo 2023

Problem Statement

This project involved launching a new international travel insurance product on MoMo's digital platform, enabling users to purchase insurance coverage seamlessly before their trips. I acted as the Business Analyst throughout the full delivery lifecycle, coordinating with internal teams and insurance partners to ensure the product met business, technical, and compliance requirements.

Solution Approach

Led comprehensive stakeholder alignment, requirement documentation, project coordination, and UAT processes to deliver end-to-end travel insurance integration with real-time partner connectivity.

Mobile Interface

Mobile app workflow showing the complete travel insurance purchase process

My Responsibilities

Stakeholder Alignment

  • Collaborated with the Product Owner to finalize user journeys and UI/UX flows.
  • Worked closely with the Business Unit to understand insurance regulations and product logic.
  • Engaged with Marketing, Risk, and Accounting teams to align on promotion rules, risk requirements, and financial reconciliation processes.
  • Coordinated with insurance partners to align on system integration and business workflows.

Requirement Documentation

  • Delivered comprehensive SRS (Software Requirements Specification) to define expected behaviors and edge cases.
  • Wrote API specifications for both internal teams (frontend/backend) and external partners to enable end-to-end system integration.

Project Coordination

  • Managed Jira tasks, defined the delivery timeline, and allocated resources across design, development, and QA teams.

UAT & Acceptance

  • Led acceptance testing to ensure all flows—from quotation to payment and policy issuance—worked correctly and matched the initial requirements.

Key Outcomes

Successfully launched the travel insurance product on MoMo's mobile app and website.

Enabled real-time integration with insurance partners, allowing instant quote retrieval and policy issuance.

Streamlined a cross-functional delivery process involving Product, Tech, Marketing, Risk, Finance, and third-party providers.
