Problem Statement

Home Credit needed to enhance their digital credit card offering by introducing key features such as NFC-based verification and Face Authentication, while optimizing the application flow across web and mobile platforms to improve user experience and security.

Solution Approach

Led cross-functional collaboration with local and international teams to deliver comprehensive requirement documentation, technical coordination, and project management for integrating biometric verification features into the credit card application process.

Mobile Interface

Home Credit Card mobile interface showing enhanced features including NFC verification and card management.
