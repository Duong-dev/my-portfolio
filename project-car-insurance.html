Car Insurance Marketplace

MoMo 2022 - 2024

Problem Statement

Users needed a digital solution to simplify car insurance purchases and claims. We built a marketplace aggregating multiple insurers, enabling users to compare prices and benefits in one place. To streamline claims, we also integrated AI-powered photo assessment tools, replacing manual inspections with instant, automated damage evaluations tailored to each insurer.

Solution Approach

Led the development of a comprehensive insurance marketplace from scratch for both mobile and web platforms, enabling seamless vehicle selection, package comparison, and purchase completion.

Web Platform Interface

Vehicle inspection guide with QR code for damage assessment

Mobile Interface

Mobile AI damage assessment workflow enabling instant vehicle inspection and claims processing

My Responsibilities

Stakeholder Collaboration

  • Acted as the key liaison between MoMo and multiple insurance partners to align on both business and technical requirements.
  • Conducted discussions on integration methods and business logic to standardize how insurers present and deliver data.

Requirement Analysis & Documentation

  • Wrote detailed SRS (Software Requirements Specification) documents and API Specifications to define product functionality and system interactions.
  • Designed APIs to support partner integration, covering quotation retrieval, policy purchase, and post-sale services.
  • Developed Sequence Diagrams to visualize end-to-end system flows and support cross-team alignment.
  • Developed Activity Diagrams to map out key decision points and end-to-end user interactions.

Project Coordination

  • Created Jira epics and tasks, defined sprint plans, and worked with developers, QA, and designers to allocate resources and align timelines.
  • Participated in daily standups, sprint planning, and backlog grooming to ensure smooth agile delivery.

UAT & Delivery

  • Defined UAT test cases and led acceptance testing for both mobile and web platforms.
  • Verified functionality against requirements and coordinated issue resolution with the tech team before launch.

Key Outcomes

Successfully launched the first version of the Car Insurance Marketplace on both app and web platforms.

Integrated with multiple major insurers, including Liberty, PVI, PTI, GIC, Bảo Việt, and MIC, to offer real-time quotations and policy comparisons.

Delivered a user-centric solution that reduced the insurance purchase process from hours to just a few minutes.
