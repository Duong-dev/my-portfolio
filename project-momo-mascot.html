MoMo Mascot Generative-AI

MoMo 2023

Problem Statement

Teams across branding, events, and product needed instant mascot visuals tailored to specific themes or contexts without waiting for designer availability. The challenge was creating consistent, high-quality MoMo mascot images that could be generated on-demand for various internal campaigns and events.

Solution Approach

Led a Generative AI initiative using Stable Diffusion to generate MoMo Mascot images based on natural language prompts. Gathered requirements from internal stakeholders, defined prompt logic and parameters for quality consistency, and coordinated integration with internal tools while collaborating with engineers for seamless deployment.
